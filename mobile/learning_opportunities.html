Education

For Children

  • Grades 6-9, Sunday School at 9:00 a.m.
  • Age 3 - Grade 5, Sunday School at 10:00 a.m. (during worship)

In addition to Sunday School for children and youth, we also have other activities:

  • Bell Choir for children up to 6th grade. Practice is held during the Sunday school hour, and children share their music several times a year in worship. Bells are color-coded, making it possible for even the littlest ringers to participate.
  • Middle school/High School Youth Group (grades 6-12) meets on monthly for activities or meals. Our youth regularly participate in worship and serve Christ through mission. Each year we send a team of youth for a week-long mission project.
  • Confirmation Class meets monthly for grades 8-10. We have a 3-year program that teaches the basics of being a United Methodist as well as Bible study and exploration of faith.

For Adults

  • Adult Discussion Group at 9:00 a.m. during the program year (Sept. through May). A recent topic was "God in the News."
  • Short term Bible Studies
  • Periodic topical forums

New Member Classes are held periodically for people who would like to become members. Please call the church office if you would like to attend.

For more information on these opportunities, please contact the church office.
