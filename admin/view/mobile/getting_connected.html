Getting Connected

Getting connected at Silver Lake is about forming relationships that are deeper and more supporting than what is often possible in other areas of our lives. Whether the focus is learning, serving or purely social, members value diversity and encourage all to develop their individual faith in an affirming atmosphere.

The Silver Lake Women's Group meets monthly to plan outreach and fellowship events. See This Week at Silver Lake for their current activities.

United Methodist Women is a study and service group that supports United Methodist causes worldwide.

The Silver Lake Methodist Men's Group meets on the last Saturday of the month at 8:00 AM for fellowship and breakfast at a local restaurant. See the calendar for this month's location.

Silver Lake Vets and Friends Not just for veterans, this group plans and hosts a number of fun and meaningful events each year.

Book Groups are offered weekly on Wednesday mornings and monthly on and Saturday afternoon.

Opportunities for Learning & Deepening in Faith are offered periodically throughout the year. Check the calendar for current offerings.
