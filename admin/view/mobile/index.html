Welcome!

Silver Lake United Methodist Church is a welcoming group of people of all ages. We celebrate the Christian faith in contemporary ways and encourage each other to become involved in making the world more loving and just.

If you're looking for a church home that lets you think for yourself while engaging with and helping others, Silver Lake may be worth a closer look.

Thanks for visiting our web site. We hope that you will also visit our church in person sometime soon.
